# MobileTabs 移动端标签页

> 业务组件 / Domain-specific business components for ERP scenarios.

移动端标签页 — 面向 ERP/企业场景的业务组件。
MobileTabs — a business component for ERP / enterprise scenarios.

## 何时使用 / When to Use

需要移动端标签页时使用本组件。用于移动端触控与窄屏场景；注意手势、安全区与大号点击热区。

Use MobileTabs when you need: MobileTabs: enterprise business component — see Storybook for interactive demos. Use for touch and narrow screens; prioritize large hit targets and safe areas.
