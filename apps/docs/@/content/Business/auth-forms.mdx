# AuthForms 认证表单

> 业务组件 / Domain-specific business components for ERP scenarios.

认证表单 — 面向 ERP/企业场景的业务组件。
AuthForms — a business component for ERP / enterprise scenarios.

## 何时使用 / When to Use

登录、注册、找回密码等认证相关表单。

Authentication forms for sign-in, sign-up, and password recovery.
