## API / Props

### Customer360Card

| Prop | Type | Default | 说明 |
|---|---|---|---|
| `className` | `string` | — | 自定义样式类名 |
| `name` | `string` | — | Customer name. |
| `logo` | `string` | — | Customer logo URL. |
| `industry` | `string` | — | Industry classification. |
| `tier` | `"platinum" \| "gold" \| "silver" \| "...` | — | Customer tier/level. |
| `healthScore` | `number` | — | Health score 0–100. |
| `metrics` | `CustomerMetric[]` | — | Key metrics. |
| `contacts` | `CustomerContact[]` | — | Contact persons. |
| `activities` | `CustomerActivity[]` | — | Recent activities. |
| `lifetimeValue` | `number` | — | Total lifetime value. |
| `currencySymbol` | `string` | `¥` | Currency symbol. |
| `tags` | `string[]` | — | Tags. |
| ...rest | `React.HTMLAttributes` | — | 透传原生 DOM 属性 |
